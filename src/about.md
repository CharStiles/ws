Char Stiles is a computational artist, educator, and researcher. She works
creatively in the lower levels of graphical computational systems & makes
jokes about how computers work.

She was at the MIT Media Lab's Future Sketches group researching the future
of creative coding in performance. She is a part of the
[Livecode.nyc](https://livecode.nyc) collective & she co-founded Hex House,
an artist studio and event space in East Williamsburg.

She has performed internationally, including festivals such as Electric
Forest, Portola, and Mutek Nexus. She has lectured and led workshops at
Carnegie Mellon University, Duke University, University of Limerick, MIT,
Cooper Union, The New School and NYU.
